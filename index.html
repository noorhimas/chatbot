What Is Network Operations Center?

Network Operations Center (NOC) is a centralized team responsible for monitoring, managing, and maintaining an organization’s IT and network infrastructure. It ensures 24/7 uptime, resolves incidents quickly, and improves overall network performance while reducing operational burden.
